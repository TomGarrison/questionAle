How To Play

Questionale is a group trivia drinking game.

There are no teams, and everyone must work together to correctly answer questions that appear on the screen.

When you and your friends select a correct answer, you will automatically move forward to the next question.

When you answer a question incorrectly, a frosty mug will appear on the screen and all players will have to drink until the mug disappears.

Questions get easier the more you drink, so this game will provide a long night of fun. Choose a trivia category on begin!
